Weight Lifting

Weight lifting is one of the most healthiest and fittest forms of exercise It strengthens your muscles, legs, and improves aerobic activity over time One should be careful before lifting heavier weight and consult a gym trainer! Want to get strong fast this is for you!

Boxing

Boxing is another crucial exercise form of exercise, during boxing one hits a boxing bag or trains with another person and you can also become a more agile person. So boxing overall improves strength and speed.

Track and Field

Who loves to run? This exercise is for you! During track and field, one runs around between the colored white lines and jumps through obstacles or race against other people. Track and field improves cardiovascular strength, leg muscles, and agility.

Swimming

If you love the water, swimming must me your thing! When you swim, you work a lot of muscles at the same time. You splash hard in the water and make it to the other side of the pool. Make sure a coach and lifeguard is nearby to help you out!
